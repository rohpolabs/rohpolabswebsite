Praya is a wake-up call and voice alarm application that:

  • Simulates incoming phone calls at user-scheduled times on your device's lock screen

8. Alarm Reliability Disclaimers

8.1 Factors Outside Our Control

Alarm delivery depends on factors outside our control. Alarms may not fire if:

  • Your device is powered off or restarting
  • Battery optimization settings are killing the App's background processes
  • Your device manufacturer (Xiaomi, Samsung, Huawei, OnePlus, etc.) has aggressive memory management that terminates background apps
  • Your device is in Do Not Disturb mode set to block all interruptions
  • Android's alarm quota limits have been reached on your device
  • Your device's system clock has been changed
  • You have not granted all required permissions
  • Available device storage is critically low

8.2 Our Recommendation

We strongly recommend keeping a secondary backup alarm for any critical wake-up times, including work, travel, medical, or other time-sensitive commitments. Praya is designed to complement your morning routine, not replace safety-critical alarms.

8.3 Permission Requirements

For reliable alarm delivery, the following are strongly recommended:

  • Battery optimization exemption granted for Praya
  • Exact alarm permission granted (Android 12+)
  • Full screen intent permission granted (Android 14+)
  • Autostart permission enabled (Xiaomi, Huawei devices)

Instructions for granting these are provided within the App.
