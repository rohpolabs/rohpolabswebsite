# Privacy Policy - TaskMaster Pro

Last updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

For TaskMaster Pro, we collect:
- Task data (titles, descriptions, due dates, categories, priorities)
- User account information (email, username when you create an account)
- Device information for compatibility and performance
- Usage analytics (anonymized)
- Performance data (app crashes, response times, technical diagnostics)
- Storage data (local app settings, preferences, cached data stored on your device)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for legitimate purposes that are clearly explained and necessary for providing our services:
- Provide core app functionality
- Sync data across your devices when you enable these features
- Personalize user experience
- Save your preferences and settings
- Improve app performance and fix bugs and issues
- Maintain app security and regular security updates

3. Data Sharing

We share your information in limited circumstances:
- With your consent when you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
- With trusted third-party services necessary to operate our apps (e.g., cloud storage, authentication)
- When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
- In connection with business transfers (with proper notice)

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:
- Industry-standard encryption for data transmission and at rest
- Secure OAuth2 authentication with Google and Apple
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Secure cloud storage with encryption at rest
- Privacy by design - minimal necessary data collection

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
- Request a copy of your personal data that we hold
- Request correction of inaccurate information that we hold
- Request deletion of your personal data and associated data permanently
- Request a portable copy of your data in a machine-readable format (JSON, CSV)
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Object to our processing of your personal information in certain circumstances
- Withdraw consent where legally permitted

To exercise these rights, please contact us at:

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for providing our services:
- You can delete your account and we will remove all associated data within 30 days
