Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026 | Effective Date: January 18, 2026

Introduction

At RohpoLabs, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy explains how we protect your information across all our mobile applications.

By downloading, accessing, or using our applications, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our apps.

No Account Required

Our applications are designed to work without requiring you to create an account, register, or provide any personal information. You can use all features of our apps without:

  • No Registration: No sign-up or account creation process
  • No Login: No username or password required
    • No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect any of the following information:

  • No Account Information: We do not collect usernames, passwords, email addresses, or profile information
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or any other personal details
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not track how you use our applications beyond what is necessary for basic functionality
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or store your geographic location
  • No Contact Information: We do not access your contacts or address book
  • No Biometric Data: We do not collect fingerprints, face ID, or other biometric information

Local Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. When you use our applications:

  • Your app data, settings, preferences, and any content you create is stored only on your device
  • We do not have access to your device or the data stored on it
  • We do not sync your data to any cloud service or server
  • You have full control over your data at all times

No Data Sharing

We do NOT share your information with anyone because we do not collect it in the first place. Specifically:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties
  • No Advertising Partners: We do not share your information with advertisers or ad networks
  • No Analytics Services: We do not use third-party analytics services that track your behavior
  • No Data Selling: We do not sell your data to anyone, ever

Automatic Data Collection

We may collect the following technical information automatically:

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Device Information: General device type (Android/iOS) and operating system version for compatibility
  • App Performance: Technical diagnostics to improve app functionality

This information is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we do not collect your personal information, you have complete control over your data:

  • Full Control: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete Anytime: You can delete the app and all your data at any time
  • No Tracking: We do not track your behavior across different apps or services
  • Opt-Out Not Needed: Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to opt-out from

Children's Privacy

Our services are suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Our apps comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations
  • No account creation means no personal information is at risk

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect our applications:

  • No Remote Access: We cannot access your device or your data remotely
  • Local Storage: Your data is protected by your device's security features
  • App Sandboxing: App data is isolated within the app's secure sandbox
  • Secure Transmission: Any data transmitted (for crash reports) uses encrypted connections

While we take reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • In-app notification (for significant changes)

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
